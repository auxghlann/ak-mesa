# Prism Portfolio System

## Visual Identity
A design system inspired by Google’s Material You, focusing on high legibility, generous whitespace, and friendly, rounded interactions.

## Color Palette

### Surface & Background
- **Surface:** `#f8f9fa` (Light grey for main backgrounds)
- **Surface Container:** `#ffffff` (Pure white for cards and elevated sections)
- **Surface Dim:** `#d9dadb` (Subtle borders and dividers)

### Accents
- **Primary:** `#4285f4` (Google Blue - used for primary CTAs and active states)
- **Secondary:** `#34a853` (Google Green - used for success states and accents)
- **Tertiary:** `#ea4335` (Google Red - used for highlights and error states)
- **Quaternary:** `#fbbc05` (Google Yellow - used for secondary accents)

### Text & Labels
- **On-Surface:** `#202124` (Deep grey/black for high-contrast body text)
- **On-Surface Variant:** `#5f6368` (Medium grey for secondary text and labels)

## Typography (Manrope)
A modern, geometric sans-serif that balances the friendliness of Google's Product Sans with high scannability.

- **Headline Large:** 32pt / Bold (Hero titles)
- **Headline Medium:** 24pt / Semi-Bold (Section headers)
- **Body Large:** 16pt / Regular (Main copy)
- **Label Large:** 14pt / Medium (Buttons and Chips)
- **Label Small:** 12pt / Regular (Metadata and small labels)

## Component Specifications

### Cards
- **Border Radius:** `24px` (Full roundness)
- **Shadow:** `0px 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)` (Flat elevation)
- **Padding:** `24px` (Standard desktop padding)

### Buttons
- **Shape:** Pill (Full rounded ends)
- **Primary:** Background `Primary`, Text `White`
- **Secondary:** Bordered, Text `Primary`
- **Interaction:** Subtle scale down (95%) on press.

### Chips & Tags
- **Background:** `Surface Container Low`
- **Shape:** `Rounded-Full`
- **Text:** `On-Surface Variant` (Medium contrast)
